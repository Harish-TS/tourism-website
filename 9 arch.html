Nine Arch Bridge - Bridge In The Sky :-

Nine Arch Bridge is also called the Bridge in the sky and is among the iconic Sri Lanka tourism places. It is in the small mountain town of Ella and offers visitors with splendid view of the
surrounding area which comprises of lush greenery and tea fields. What makes the bridge unique is that it is made up of stone, brick and cement. There is no use of any steel. It is one
of the best places to visit in Sri Lanka for a memorable holiday.This bridge is between demodara railway station and Ella is has become one of the major tourist hotspots of Sri Lanka and it
is largely due to the epic scenery and hikes in the region such as Ella Rock and Little Adam's Peak.To Know More Click Here
